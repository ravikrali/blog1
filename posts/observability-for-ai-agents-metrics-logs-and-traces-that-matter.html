Observability for AI Agents: Metrics, Logs, and Traces that Matter

A practical telemetry blueprint for supervising agent behavior, detecting drift, and proving value with measurable outcomes.

The pillars

Metrics (performance + business outcomes), logs (structured + redacted), and traces (distributed, end-to-end). Add agent-specific telemetry: confidence scores and model/version lineage.

Operational dashboards

Track: task success rate, time-to-complete, first-time acceptance rate, prevented policy violations, cost avoidance, and MTTR for escalations.

Drift and hallucination controls

Monitor confidence drops, repeated retries, unusual access patterns, and mismatches between generated artifacts and the knowledge base. Build escalation paths into the platform.

Implementation notes

Use this as a reference pattern, then tailor it to your enterprise boundaries, risk tiers, and standards library. The goal is repeatable execution with measurable governance.
