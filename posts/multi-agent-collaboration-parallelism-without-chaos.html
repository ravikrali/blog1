Multi-Agent Collaboration: Parallelism Without Chaos

Patterns for coordinating specialist agents—sequential, parallel, hierarchical—so complex work completes faster with fewer errors.

Choose the right collaboration model

Sequential pipelines work for linear flows. Parallel execution accelerates decomposable tasks. Hierarchical orchestration handles complex trade-offs and conflict resolution.

Specialization wins

Domain-specific agents (integration, testing, documentation, compliance) improve quality and reduce rework. The orchestrator’s job is to manage handoffs and context.

Conflict management

Define precedence rules, use validation agents, and rely on policy gates. Human approval remains the final authority for business-critical decisions.

Implementation notes

Use this as a reference pattern, then tailor it to your enterprise boundaries, risk tiers, and standards library. The goal is repeatable execution with measurable governance.
